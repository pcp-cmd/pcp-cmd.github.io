# Aleksi Lab v1.2.4 — Article field + dark card palette refinement

## Fixes

1. **Article dark field expanded**
   - Increased `.article-shell` max width and added horizontal padding.
   - The side rails no longer sit exactly on the edge of the dark region.
   - Added subtle left/right hairlines, like a large Claude-style dark reading panel.

2. **Card color material refined**
   - Cards now use Claude palette as quiet material tint, hairline, and border temperature.
   - Removed the feeling of colored dark blocks while preserving clay, ochre, sage, blue, and violet semantics.
   - Hover stays restrained: no glow, no shadow, no light pollution.

3. **Article manuscript and glyph surfaces refined**
   - Manuscript panel gets a warmer graphite surface.
   - Glyph card uses a muted blue temperature similar to Claude's quiet illustrative surfaces.
