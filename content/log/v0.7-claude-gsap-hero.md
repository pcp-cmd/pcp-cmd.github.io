# v0.7 Claude GSAP Hero Refinement

## Change

Restored the first-viewport signal to `Aleksi Lab`, kept `未完手稿` as the manuscript spirit name, and rebuilt the hero as a neural manuscript glyph.

## Decisions

- Use a Claude-inspired warm neutral palette: paper, graphite ink, oat structure, and clay signal.
- Keep typography in the Anthropic-style serif/sans pairing with stable local fallbacks.
- Add a Plog Ledger section so revision notes, essay seeds, and design logs are visible on the homepage.
- Use GSAP timelines with labels for the opening rhythm: copy, paper, ink, connection, activation.
- Keep motion on transforms, opacity, and SVG stroke drawing for better mobile performance.

## Reason

The previous hero technically used GSAP, but the visual effect still felt too static and too card-like. This version makes the hero feel like a living research manuscript without turning it into a dashboard or a generic AI interface.
