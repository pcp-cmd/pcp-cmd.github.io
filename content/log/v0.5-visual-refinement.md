# v0.5 Visual Refinement

## Change

Refined the homepage color system and hero title sizing after visual review.

## Decisions

- Reduce the hero title size and prevent Chinese title wrapping.
- Remove the heavy grid-like paper background.
- Reduce terracotta usage to anchor moments only.
- Make cards cleaner, flatter, and closer to a quiet manuscript surface.

## Reason

The v0.4 narrative structure was useful, but the palette felt muddy and the oversized Chinese title broke the visual rhythm.
